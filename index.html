THE MENTAL HEALTH LANDSCAPE: PAKISTAN

Pakistan is currently facing a silent mental health emergency. With over 50 million individuals affected and a staggering 90% treatment gap, Sukoon-e-Nau was established to bridge this divide. By providing anonymous, clinical-grade screening, we aim to transform how mental health is accessed and understood across the nation.
